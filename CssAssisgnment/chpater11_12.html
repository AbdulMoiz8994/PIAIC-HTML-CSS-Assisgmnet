PIAIC

PRESENDTIAL INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence & Computing (PIAIC) is Privately non-profit educational initiative by President of Pakistan Dr.Arif Alvi,Kazi Rahat Ali, and Zia khan to help promote education,reserach and busniess tech fields like

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artifical Intelligence
  • Block-chain
  • Internet of Things

Cloud Native Computing:

The Cloud Native Computing program is to prepare to students in an open source technologies like, Linux,Docker,Kubernetes,JavaScript,Node.js,React.js and React-Native while introducing the students to development Microservices and Software devlopment technique on cloud-platfrom like AWS

Artifical Intelligence

The artifical intelligence program of PIAIC takes the students through the foundation of AI technology via teaching numpy,python,pandas,tensorflow 2.0 flask ,and teaching deploy AI solutions and use cases, and adding with real-life examples of modern day AI practice.

Blockchain

Blockchain is a Bitcoin block explorer service, as well as a cryptocurrency wallet and a cryptocurrency exchange supporting Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, and Ethereum . They also provide Bitcoin data charts, stats, and market information. In the simplest terms, Blockchain can be described as a data structure that holds transactional records and while ensuring security, transparency, and decentralization.

Internet of Things

The Internet of things describes the network of physical objects—“things”—that are embedded with sensors, Rust,Embaded Rust,Kubernetes software, and other technologies for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with other devices and systems over the Internet.
